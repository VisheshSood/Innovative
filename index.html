Operations

Innovative Gloves has embarked on research and formulation of polymer applications in the rubber gloves industry. Our formulated products have been widely accepted by customers as we aspire to continue providing innovative solutions to delight our customers. There are several reasons to our success in the glove industry. Some of the reasons are listed here:

Onsite Tensile & Elongation Testing


The key reason to the success of our unbreakable nitrile gloves is the fact that we carry out extensive tensile and elongation tests on all our products. These tests truly measure the strength of our gloves and help us find better ways to make sure our gloves remain as powerful as they are.

Research And Development


Innovative Gloves believes that research and development is a key component in the success of a product. We partake in thorough research and testing to ensure that we create innovative gloves, just as our name states. Our research has allowed us to be one of the best upcoming companies for years, and will continue to do so.

Advanced Post Aging Testing


At Innovative Gloves, we believe it is vital to make sure that our gloves last as long as possible, so that a client knows they are about to wear a sanitized and safe pair of gloves to protect themselves from all sorts of messes, regardless of age. We carry out several tests to ensure our gloves are as safe to use regardless of their age.

Clean Technology


Here at Innovative Gloves, we know the importance of sanitized gloves in the daily use of mechanics, doctors, household members, and several other users. That is why we set a very high standard of cleanliness when it comes to making our gloves. This is one reason we carry several certifications from around the world, and have a very loyal customer base.

Certifications

Innovative Gloves has been certified under the ISO 9001 quality management system which is crucial to fostering acceptance in the world's markets.

ISO 13485:2003

Innovative Gloves demonstrates its ability to provide medical devices and related services that consistently meet customer requirements and regulatory requirements applicable to medical devices and related services.

ISO 9001:2008 Certified

Innovative Gloves demonstrates its ability to consistently provide product that meets customer and applicable statutory requirements, and aims to enhance customer satisfaction through the effective application of processes for continual improvement of the system and the assurance of conformity to customer.

U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)

Innovative Gloves has been audited by US FDA inspectors in 2002, and are also certified for maintaining an excellent GMP system.

Conformité Européene (CE)

Innovative Gloves declares that the product complies with the essential requirements of the relevant European health, safety and environmental protection legislation, in practice by many of the so-called Product Directives.

Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA)

Innovative Gloves produces products that have 510K approvals on all gloves making us TGA compliant.

Canadian Medical Devices Regulations (CMDCAS)

Innovative Gloves has proven that we provide gloves that are safe, effective and meet the quality standards set by Canada's Medical Devices Regulations (MDR) Department.

CSR-DIW Award Holders

Innovative Gloves were presented with the well recognized CSE-DIW Award in 2013.
